About Us

Discover our identity, mission, and calling

Who We Are

Joyful Assembly Ministry is a Bible-believing church committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and nurturing spiritual growth.

We are driven by the mandate to win souls, disciple believers, and demonstrate the love of Christ in our community.

Our foundation is built on prayer, worship, and the Word of God.

Our Vision & Mission

Why We Exist

Our Vision

To raise a generation of believers who passionately pursue Christ and impact their communities through the Gospel.

Our Mission

To evangelize, disciple, equip, and empower people to fulfill God's purpose through worship, prayer, and biblical teaching.

Our Core Values

What We Stand For

Faith

Living by faith and trusting God's promises.

Prayer

Building a strong prayer culture.

Worship

Honoring God through heartfelt worship.

Evangelism

Reaching souls with the Gospel of Christ.

Joyful Assembly Ministry is committed to preaching Christ, discipling believers and impacting communities through the Gospel.
